Do you have an idea for an innovative new classroom project or event, but don't have all the funding you need? MichMATYC now offers grants for members of up to $500 in support of activities of membership related to innovative programs, events or projects that will significantly enhance a students' classroom experience or for professional development shared among regional institutions. Check out the [application page](https://docs.google.com/a/swmich.edu/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd2bZPDtyGPZMrQf1TYjjF49lGC037V3yygPozVifNPwk5PZA/viewform) for more information!
