Welcome to MichMATYC!

Welcome to the website of the Michigan Mathematical Association of Two-Year Colleges! MichMATYC is an affiliate of the American Mathematical Association of Two Year Colleges (AMATYC) – the national organization devoted soley to issues facing mathematics educators in the first two years of college. Since its beginnings in 1981, a major focus of MichMATYC's mission has been professional development, which includes our annual conference in October each year. Here you can learn about our history, become a member, explore member resources, read our newsletter, visit links to professional organizations, and much more!
