Welcome to MichMATYC!

Welcome to the website of the Michigan Mathematical Association of Two-Year Colleges. We hope that you find this site useful and informative, where you will learn about our history, can become a member, and much more! MichMATYC is an affiliate of the American Mathematical Association of Two Year Colleges (AMATYC). You will also find information about our organization, member resources, links to professional organizations, newsletter, and job board.
