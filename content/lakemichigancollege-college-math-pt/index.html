+++ date = "2019-12-17T19:28:00+00:00" layout = "post" showdate = false title = "Job Board" +++ ### Lake Michigan College #### College Mathematics - Part-Time Faculty
**Position Information**
College Mathematics – Part-Time Faculty (Continuous Applicant Pool)
Salary Range: $36 per hour
Department: Arts and Sciences Education
Reports to: Department Chair, Mathematics

**Job Summary** Uses technology based instruction to sustain the specific courses and curricula mandated by the educational mission and goals of the College.

**Position Accountabilities and Essential Functions** Under the direct supervision of the Department Chair, each adjunct faculty member is responsible for satisfying the educational, administrative, and fiscal requirements inherent in his/her position.
  • Instructs students in assigned classes.
  • Provides academic guidance and counseling to students in cooperation with Student Services Personnel.
  • Maintains academic standards.
  • Prepares course syllabus and outline.
  • Monitors student progress.
  • Keeps adequate and accurate records.
  • Enters final course grades online.
**Other Duties** Other duties as assigned.

**Job Specifications** Master’s degree or higher in relevant discipline or subfield or Master’s degree and 18 graduate hours in relevant discipline or subfield.

Special Instructions to Applicants: By completing the application, your information will be added to the candidate pool. Should review of your qualifications result in a decision to pursue your candidacy, you will be contacted.
If Lake Michigan College chooses to pursue your candidacy, please note that official transcripts for all degrees will be required for immediate receipt upon hire.
