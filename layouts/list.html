Conferences

An opportunity to share ideas, consolidate resources, and plan for the future needs of community colleges in the state of Michigan is one of MichMATYC's primary purposes. The organization has two conferences and meetings each year – one in October hosted by one of our community colleges, and another in April in partnership with the Michigan Section of the Mathematical Association of America (MAA).

For more information regarding our annual conferences and meetings, please see the following future and previous conferences.
